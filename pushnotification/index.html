Push notifications feature

Setup

Becarful, this setup is not the real setup that our client have to deploy. It's a quick setup to play with the feaure.

1. Create your profile on the database

In to the profile table "A*****PushNotification", create your profile with your email address.

2. Set the userId

Go back to this page. In the url parameter "userId", replace "XXXXXX" with your email address.

3. Click on the ring bell

Check the state of you subscription. We must be unsubscribe.

4. Delete the cache of your browser

It's just a precaution.

5. Reload the web page and subscribe

After reloading the page, the pop-in to subscribe to push notification appears

6. Now, you can sent push

From the push notification platform or from the scenario.
